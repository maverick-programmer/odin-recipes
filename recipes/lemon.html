Description

This Lemon Garlic Roast Chicken is a simple yet deliciously aromatic dish. The lemon and garlic infuse the chicken with bright, flavorful notes, while the skin turns crispy and golden. It’s a comforting meal, perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
  • lemons
  • garlic cloves
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • tablespoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Rinse and pat dry the chicken, then place it in a roasting pan.
  3. Juice one lemon and mix it with olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub this mixture all over the chicken, making sure to get some under the skin.
  5. Cut the second lemon into quarters and place them inside the chicken cavity.
  6. Roast for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, until the skin is golden and the juices run clear.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before carving and serving.
