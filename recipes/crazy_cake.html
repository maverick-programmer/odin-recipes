Description

This cake was popular during the Great Depression, and does not have eggs in it. It is a very moist and chocolatey cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add vinegar, vanilla extract, vegetable oil, and water. Mix until well combined. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
