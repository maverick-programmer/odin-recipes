Chocolate Brownies

Description

hese chocolate brownies are rich, fudgy, and perfectly chocolatey. With a slightly crispy crust and a gooey center, t hey’re ideal for satisfying your sweet cravings.This recipe is simple and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 150 g butter
  • 200 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and butter a square baking pan.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a double boiler or microwave.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the sugar and eggs until the mixture becomes pale.
  4. Add the melted chocolate, flour, vanilla, and salt, then mix until you get a smooth batter.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before cutting into squares
