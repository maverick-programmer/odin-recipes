Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian dish, rich and creamy, made with eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, guanciale (or pancetta), and black pepper. Itâ€™s a quick and flavorful meal, perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 150 g guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • garlic cloves
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the guanciale or pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix them with the Pecorino Romano.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Toss the pasta with the guanciale, then remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg-cheese mixture to create a creamy sauce. Add a bit of cooking water if needed.
  6. Serve immediately with freshly ground black pepper and extra Pecorino.
