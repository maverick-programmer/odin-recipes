Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a timeless classic, made with crispy croutons, fresh Parmesan, and romaine lettuce, all tossed in a creamy garlic and anchovy dressing. It’s a refreshing and flavorful starter, perfect as a side dish or a light main course.

Ingredients

  • head of romaine lettuce
  • cup croutons
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • garlic clove
  • tablespoon lemon juice
  • teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • anchovy fillet (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce leaves, then tear them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, mustard, and anchovy (if using) to make the dressing.
  3. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large salad bowl, combine the lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
